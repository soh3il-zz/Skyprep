About

The Story

In 2008, the SkyPrep founders were approached by a colleague to find them an online testing and training software to sell courses online. They were amazed they couldn’t find an online training software platform that was easy to use, yet had all the functionality that they were looking for! So what did they do? They built one.

Since then, the core value at SkyPrep has remained the same; create software that is simple, yet powerful. SkyPrep has so far received a tremendous response from all types of people using the online training platform.

Whether it’s a company with over 1000 employees to train, a small-sized business with just a few employees , a private tutor, a large school, or someone wanting to launch an e-commerce platform, the feedback has been unanimous; SkyPrep is addictively easy to use.

Since its inception, SkyPrep training software has registered over 10,000 users. Now, we’re ready to let anyone use our training software with our new training platform- from anywhere in the world! Learning how our online training software can work for you.
