Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why should I choose SkyPrep?

    You’re here to build your training program. You’re not here to get frustrated trying to learn HOW to build your training program. Unquestionably, some training software out there may have more features then we provide, but that’s by design. We only implement the features that the vast majority of people (90% or more) are looking for in their training software so that we can ensure a streamlined, pain-free experience. That means less time and effort learning how to use our platform, and more time spent actually training and being productive. Most importantly, we guarantee that your learners will know how to use the platform within minutes of logging in, or we’ll personally train them.

    What is an active user?

    An active user can login as many times as they want during the billing cycle and are given full access to their courses. At anytime you can de-active a user, and they won’t count towards your active user quota for the next billing cycle. If the user logs in, their account will be re-actived and count towards your quota.

  • How’s your pricing different then your competitors?

    Well, we’re significantly more affordable than most all of our competitors. Our program is extremely effective, with a fair pricing model. We only charge you based on the number of learners that actually use your platform each month (i.e. the number of active users). You can have an unlimited number of people enrolled into your system, but you only pay for the ones who are actually logging in that particular month. With SkyPrep, you only pay for what you use. Pick a plan that suits the number of estimated users you will have per month, but you can always switch between plans.

    What if I exceed the number of users in my plan?

    If your users increase, we’ll automatically notify you to to switch to the best plan. You you can either switch between plans for the month or you can choose to pay the additional fee per user.
