Upload Once. Use everywhere.

Upload Training Materials

This is the content that will educate your learners. Go to your Material Manager to upload PDFs, Word and Excel documents, PowerPoints, Videos and much more. Once uploaded you can organize your files into folders ready for use any number of times in your courses.

Create Exams

From your Exam Manager you create your exams. These exams include various question types such as multiple choice, true/false, fill in the blank and essay format. Any exam you create, is ready for use in any of your courses.

Build Courses

Use your Course Manager to pick-and-choose from any of your documents and exams to build an entire course. You can arrange these items in any order you want. You’re done making a course. Simple.

Manage Users. Track Performance

Add Users Individually or in Bulk

SkyPrep allows you to add users individually or in bulk. Removing users is just as easy.

Group and Manage Users

You can manage and organize users in anyway you’d like by placing them into various groups. You can enroll individual users or whole groups into courses. Managing users has never been so easy

Track and Analyze

With our analytic and simple-to-use reporting tools, you’ll be able to download reports and track user performance and grades at anytime. You can monitor individuals, groups and courses.

Brand your portal. Select your own domain

SkyPrep allows you to customize your training platform by uploading your own logo and selecting your own domain. Your can select a SkyPrep sub-domain, for example YourCompany.skyprep.com If you really want to personalize the feel of your training portal, you can use your own domain, for example training.YourCompany.com, creating a true white label solution.
